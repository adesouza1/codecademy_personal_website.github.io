About Me

I initially obtained my Bacholers in Biology with a specialization in Neuroscience from the University of Maryland, College Park before continuing on to the University of California, San Diego to pursue a Ph.D in Neuroscience. During my experience as a neuroscientist I taught myself some basic coding skills first using MatLab and then moving on to Python out of necessity in order to be able to analyze and process code. Then early on in my neuroscience Ph.D I discovered Bitcoin as well as smart contract blockchains such as Ethereum. I became enamored with blockchains and spent most of my free time learning about them and exploring how to use them. After saving some money and leaving my Ph.D, I began decided to spend a year becoming much more proficent at coding so I could work in the blockchain industry in order to build interesting and valuable products. This website will document this journey.
